How to Play

Build a chain of compound words. Each word connects to the next.

CAMP+ FIRE+ WORK+ OUT

  • The first letter of each word is always shown.
  • In Easy mode, every word has a clue visible.
  • In Hard mode, only the first word's clue is shown.
  • You have 3 hints — each reveals one more letter.
  • 5 wrong guesses ends the game.
  • One new daily puzzle per day, same for everyone.
  • Past dailies and bonus puzzles are always playable in the Archive.
  • Only today's daily counts toward your stats.
